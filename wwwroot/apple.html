Access your email on an Apple device

  1. Install Microsoft Outlook from the Apple App Store.
  2. Open Outlook on your phone once it has installed.
  3. When prompted for your email address, type your full Living Sky email address.
  4. A new window will appear with the Living Sky logo on it, prompting you for your password.
  5. Enter your password - this is the same password you would use to sign into a school computer, or your Living Sky Google account.
  6. You should now be logged into your email on your Apple device.
